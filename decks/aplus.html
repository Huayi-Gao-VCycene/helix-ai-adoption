How It Works
Three portals. One ecosystem.
Every player in the delivery chain gets their own purpose-built interface.
Business Portal Dispatcher dashboard: Create delivery orders, assign drivers, optimize routes automatically, view all deliveries live on a map. Send customers branded tracking links in one click.
Driver Portal Driver mobile app: Accept jobs, get turn-by-turn navigation, collect proof of delivery (photo + signature + timestamp). Drivers look professional — even independent operators.
Customer Portal Real-time tracking: Customers get a live tracking link — ETA, driver location, delivery confirmation. No more "where's my order?" calls to the business.
Hybrid Mode Overflow delivery: No driver available? Businesses request from our managed driver network at a per-delivery rate — seamlessly within the same platform.
