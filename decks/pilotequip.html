The Hidden Wall

Equipment: The Cost Nobody Budgets For

Headsets
$300–$900 new. The single largest equipment purchase — and most students buy new because they don't know an alternative exists.
Flight Bags
$80–$200 new. Required from day one. Carries charts, logbook, kneeboard, headset. Every pre-flight needs it.
Charts & Tools
$100+ per cycle. Kneeboards, protractors, navigation charts, plotter. Bought new. Most items are durable and reusable.

Graduating pilots leave behind perfectly functional gear with no organized buyer. Student pilots need that gear with no organized seller. PilotEquip closes the gap.

Product
HELIX Pitch Day
The Process

From Graduating Pilot to New Student

Acquire
Purchase surplus from pilots who've completed training, flight schools, and aviation estates. Competitive buy prices incentivize sellers. Fully documented provenance.
Inspect
Every item tested against manufacturer specs: headset audio quality and seal, bag structural integrity, tool accuracy. Items that don't pass are not listed — ever.
Prepare
Cleaned, sanitized, photographed from multiple angles, and graded A/B/C. Grade and condition shown transparently to every buyer.
Sell
Listed on online store with full condition disclosure. Shipped directly to student pilots across Canada with a satisfaction guarantee.
